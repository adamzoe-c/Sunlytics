How It Works
Your Mission, Step by Step
Simple. Automated. Profitable. No experience with solar needed.
1
One Time

Register & Get Verified

Fill out the form below with your region, drone model, and CAA license. We verify your credentials and add you to our pilot database. This takes 24–48 hours.

2
Automated Alert

Receive a Mission Near You

When a client in your area orders a drone report, you get an SMS and email: "New mission at [Address]. Reward: €100. Accept?" You choose to accept or decline — no pressure, no commitment.

3
On Site

Fly the Pre-Planned Route

Drive to the location. Open the Sunlytics mission on your phone. The flight route is already planned for you — you just press Start. Your drone maps the roof automatically and lands.

4
Upload

Upload the Raw Images

Go home, log into your pilot dashboard, and upload the photo dataset. Our system automatically checks image quality and completeness. No editing, no engineering, no client contact.

5
Paid

Get Paid Automatically

Once images are validated, your €100 is released automatically to your bank account or Revolut. No invoicing, no chasing. Usually within a few hours of upload.

Requirements
What You Need
We keep it simple — if you have a license and a drone, you likely qualify

You Need

Valid CAA Drone License

Latvian Civil Aviation Agency certification required. Open or Specific category accepted.

Compatible Drone

DJI Mini 3 Pro or above. Must support waypoint/automated flight missions.

Smartphone

Android or iPhone to run the Sunlytics mission app and upload images.

Based in Latvia or Baltic Region

We prioritize pilots who can reach missions within 30 minutes.

You Do NOT Need

Solar Knowledge

You just collect images. Our AI handles all the analysis.

Client Communication

You never speak to the client. All coordination is through our platform.

Photo Editing Skills

Upload raw images only. Do not edit or process anything.

Full-Time Commitment

Accept missions only when it suits you. Completely flexible.
