Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Sunlytics is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in compliance with GDPR and EU data protection laws.

1. Who We Are

Sunlytics is a solar analysis service operating in Latvia and the Baltic region. Our website is sunlytics.lv and you can contact us at info@sunlytics.lv.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Property address (for solar analysis)
  • Name, email address, and phone number (when you contact us or order a report)
  • Usage data such as pages visited and time spent on the site

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with a solar analysis result
  • To contact you regarding your drone report order
  • To respond to your inquiries
  • To improve our services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your address with our certified drone operators solely for the purpose of conducting your requested inspection. All operators are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at info@sunlytics.lv.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data State Inspectorate of Latvia

7. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to ensure proper functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at info@sunlytics.lv.
